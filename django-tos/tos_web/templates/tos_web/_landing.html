Search less, research more!

Tree of Science (ToS) is a free web base tool for science articles selection
Tos was created to enable researchers seeking scientific papers decreasing time and increasing the accuracy of the results
For more info and training, check out our youtube channel

You just need an invitation!

What is ToS?

One of ToS pillars is simplicity base on organic concept. in this way, ToS display the information of the papers in a tree shape:
Leaves are the recent articles.
Trunk are the articles that give structure.
Root are the seminal articles.
