Step 1:
Go www.webofknowledge.com and sign in. Your Institute should provide the access. If you studied or are studying at Universidad Nacional de Colombia you can access by sinab.unal.edu.co - Recursos bibliográficos - Herramientas bibliográficas - bottom next - select web of science. Finally, you need to write your email and password.

Step 2:
Change the type of data base from “All Databases” to “Web of ScienceTM Core Collection”

Step 3:
Write the keywords of your research topic. The results should be from 100 to 500 in order to identify the “Tree of Science”.

Step 4:
Download the registers. Click in the task next to “save to EndNote online” and select “save to Other File Formats”. Write the records you want to select. In number of reccors specify from 1 to the total number of registers you’ve got, In Record Content select “Full record and cited references” also in file format select “Plain Text” . Now, you will have a txt file called savedrecs.

Step 5:
Upload the saverecs.txt file to tos.manizales.unal.edu.co to generate your Tree of Science!
